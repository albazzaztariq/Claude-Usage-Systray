Claude Usage Systray — v0.1

A small always-on-top widget for Windows that displays your Claude subscription usage at a glance. It shows three percentages — session (5-hour), weekly (all models), and weekly (Sonnet) — color-coded from green to red as you approach your limits. Click it to open a detailed usage dialog with progress bars, reset timers, and extra usage tracking.

The widget sits above your taskbar and stays visible while you work. It polls the Anthropic API every 5 minutes, supports drag-to-reposition, and remembers its position between sessions. Right-click for quick access to refresh, settings, or the visual dialog. Settings include display scaling (1-400%), custom colors for usage thresholds, background color, and an option to launch on Windows startup.

This is v0.1 and has not been heavily tested. There may be bugs. If you run into issues, use the "Report an Issue" option in the right-click menu to let us know.
